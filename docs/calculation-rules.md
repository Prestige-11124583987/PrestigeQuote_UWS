# Quote Calculation Rules

1. Base door retail price = style selling price per square foot × door square feet.
2. Add-on retail price = each selected add-on's selling price × its applicable pricing driver.
3. Door-unit retail price = base door retail price + all selected add-ons.
4. Door-unit discount applies to the entire door-unit retail price.
5. Installation is calculated separately and may have its own discount.
6. Line-item package retail, discount, and package price include installation.
7. Production deposit = 50% of discounted door-unit revenue and excludes installation.

The application does not calculate or expose cost, markup, or margin information.
